About Our Lodge

Entertainment

  • Paddle boating
  • Fishing
  • Lawn area for picnics and games including horse shoes and bean bag toss
  • 3 big screens. Every football game available.
  • Kids game, tv, and game area
  • Go-Kart & Golf Cart Courses
  • Walking trails

Relaxing Evenings

  • Natural hot mineral bath
  • Beautiful South Dakota Plains scenery and sunsets
  • Gourmet dinner (optional), choose from dinner menu of steaks, ribs, seafood. Meals must be scheduled in advance
  • Campfire area conveniently located next to our very own marshmallow tree
  • Outdoor kennels provided
  • Outdoor pheasant and fish cleaning area
  • November thru March — Canada goose fly-way, we are one of their favorite stops daily. Thousands of geese stop in to the open, artesian water
