Welcome to Canada Goose Lodge

Nestled in the heart of Winner, South Dakota, Canada Goose Lodge is a private vacation log home built for those who love the outdoors. Whether you're here for world-class pheasant hunting, chasing thousands of Canadian geese on their migration route, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat in the South Dakota countryside, our lodge is your home away from home.

Winner sits at the crossroads of some of the finest upland bird hunting in North America. Our log home offers a warm, comfortable base for your group — a place to gather after a day in the field, share stories, and plan tomorrow's adventure.

Getting Here

Winner is located in south-central South Dakota along US Highway 18 and SD Highway 44. The lodge sits on Country Club Road, just minutes from downtown Winner.

From Sioux Falls: ~3 hours west on I-90 to Murdo, then south on US-83

From Rapid City: ~3.5 hours east on I-90 to Murdo, then south on US-83

From Omaha: ~5 hours north on US-281 to US-18
