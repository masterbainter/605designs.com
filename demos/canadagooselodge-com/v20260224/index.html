About Our Lodge

Canada Goose Lodge is a private vacation log home located in Winner, South Dakota — the pheasant hunting capital of the world. Whether you're here for hunting season, a family reunion, or just need a peaceful retreat on the South Dakota plains, our lodge has everything you need for an unforgettable stay.
